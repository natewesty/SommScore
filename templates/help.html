‚úÖ Understanding SommScore

Your SommScore is a daily performance metric that measures how well you're doing compared to your team's performance each day. It's calculated on a 0-100 scale, combining your sales performance and club signup achievements.

‚ú® How It's Calculated

Your daily score is determined by three key factors:

  • Sales Performance: How your daily sales compare to the team's average
    • If you're above the team average, your score increases
    • If you're below the team average, your score decreases
  • Club Signups: Each club signup adds 50 points to your daily score
  • Day Weights: Different days have different importance:
    • Weekdays (Monday-Thursday): 1.5x weight
    • Weekends (Friday-Sunday): 1.0x weight

Year Types

Two ways to track annual performance:

  • Calendar Year: January 1 - December 31
  • Fiscal Year: Customizable 12-month period (e.g., July 1 - June 30)

Daily Score Calculation

Your daily score is calculated in these steps:

  1. Calculate team's average revenue for the day
  2. Compare your revenue to team average:
    Revenue Score = ((Your Revenue - Team Average) / Team Average) √ó 50
  3. Apply day weight (1.5x for Mon-Thu, 1.0x for Fri-Sun)
  4. Add club signup bonuses (50 points each)
  5. Normalize to 0-100 scale relative to team performance

Team Setup Impact

Changes to team composition affect calculations:

  • Team averages only include Active associates
  • Rankings and comparisons are based on Active roster
  • Historical data maintains integrity by using Active status from each date
  • Status changes are reflected in real-time for new calculations

üë• Team Management

The team management system allows you to organize and track associate performance effectively:

Associate Status Types

  • Active Associates: Currently working team members whose performance is tracked and included in team calculations
  • Benched Associates: Team members temporarily not included in performance tracking (e.g., on leave, training)
  • Hidden Associates: Members who are archived but can be restored if needed

Team Organization Features

  • Drag and Drop: Easily move associates between Active and Bench status
  • Performance-Based Sorting: Active associates are automatically sorted by their SommScore
  • Quick Actions: Hide/Restore associates with one click
  • Bulk Management: View and manage all hidden associates in one place

Impact on Calculations

  • Only Active associates contribute to team averages and rankings
  • Bench and Hidden associates maintain their historical data
  • Status changes take effect immediately for future calculations
  • Historical team averages are recalculated based on who was Active on each date
